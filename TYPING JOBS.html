TYPING JOBS

#1 Amazon Mechanical Turk

Amazon Mechanical Turk (MTurk) is a marketplace for typing work that requires human intelligence. As an Amazon Mechanical Truk Worker you are free to work on the tasks you find most interesting, the tasks you enjoy completing or the tasks that pay the most.  See Also | How to Earn $15 an Hour with Amazon MTurk

How much you can make: you can expect $6-12/hour for the majority of HITs

How to redeem: through Amazon Gift Card or you can disburse your earnings to a linked bank account.

Where to Apply: Visit Amazon mTurk Worker Joining Page.

#2 LionBridge Smart Crowd

Lionbridge has more than 100,000 cloud workers from all around the world. They offer you the ability to earn money by completing small tasks that you can fit in your schedule. You work for yourself, on as many tasks as you choose, from your own device, wherever, and whenever, you want.  How much you can make: you can expect between $.40 and $.55 per 1000 keystrokes.

How to redeem: you have to earn a minimum of $50 before they pay out. Payment options are not specified on their website. Where to Apply: if you want to become an online member, visit their joining section.

 

#3 ClickWorker

Being a Clickworker is not complicated – even for a newbie. ClickWorker looking for people from anywhere in the world who enjoy writing, translating, researching, and data processing. They provide digital tasks to interested workers. How much you can make: you can expect that as a Clickworker can earn $9.00 per hour.

How to redeem: you have to earn a minimum of $50 before they pay out. Payment options are not specified on their website.

Where to Apply: You can become an online ClickWorker by visiting their joining page.

 

#4 SpeechPad

You are free to work on whatever jobs you find interesting. Find your niche, or change it up. You should fluent in English, good with grammar and be able to type at least 40 words per minute. How much you can make: you can expect generally $0.25/minute to $1.00/minute audio.

How to redeem: They pay workers in U.S. dollars through Paypal and you have to be paid every Tuesday and Friday.

Where to Apply: You can become an online Speech Pad Worker by visiting their joining page.

#5 e-Typist

E-Typist provides transcription services for hundreds of clients if you able to quickly demonstrate the ability to transcribe dictation in varying degrees of difficulty join e-Typist.

How much you can make: you can expect generally $0.15/minute to $0.50/minute audio

How to redeem: They pay workers in U.S. dollars through Paypal.

Where to Apply: You can join e-Typist by submitting a resume at employment@e-typist.com

#6 Capital Typing

Capital Typing offers a variety of services to its clients and hires freelancers or independent contractors to perform them. They are looking to hire for transcription, data entry, market research, translation, online customer support, editing and secretarial services.

How much you can make: Not specified

How to redeem: Not Specified

Where to Apply: Ask for its home-based positions directly at the Capital Typing contact us page.

#7 Data Plus+

DataPlus+ is a company that provides data entry services, database programming, and business processing that supports businesses. There are many types of work-at-home jobs that include data entry responsibilities.

How much you can make: Not specified

How to redeem: Not Specified

Where to Apply: To apply at DataPlus+, simply visit the company’s employment page and upload the required information.

#8 QuickGate or IDictate

With the Quick Gate or iDictate as an Independent Contractor, you can select your own hours that you want to work. You can work as few, or as many hours as you want.

How much you can Earn: iDictate pays one-half cent per word, but Super Rush and Multi-Speaker files are paid more.

How to redeem:  Typists are paid through PayPal every Monday.

Where to Apply: To apply at DataPlus+, simply visit the company’s employment page and upload the required information.

#9 Birch Creek Communication

Birch Creek Communication seeking people for transcription department for high-quality corporate transcriptionists, you can transcribe audio files for their corporate clients.

How much you can Earn: they pay by the audio minute, ranging from US $0.40/audio min to US $1.75/audio min. 

How to redeem:  you will be paid through PayPal.

Where to Apply: To apply at Birch Creek Communication, simply visit the company’s employment page.

#10 Working Solution

Working Solutions provide legitimate work-at-home opportunities to their agents. They hire independent contractors for both the call center and online typing or data entry jobs in the U.S. and worldwide. 

How much you can Earn: you can be earned the equivalent of anywhere from $9 an hour to $30 USD an hour.

How to Redeem: you can get paid direct deposit, or they can send checks by mail.

Where to Apply: to join Working Solution you need to visit Work from Home Job Section.

As you can see, there is a tremendous variety of opportunities to work online from home, as online typing or data entry jobs. Some require specific experience or typing speed, and others accept beginners. Study each carefully, to determine which ones suit your requirements. Also, I’d love to hear about your experience.
